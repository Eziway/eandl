Introduction

E & L Holdings is a newly established company, that uses several strategies to offer services with style by employing appropriate professional methods while striving to have a better understanding of our diverse clients. We saw a gap and went for it: The market needed a quality service provider who is super economical and offers personalized services with a smile, and our clients have been smiling ever since, after all, who can resist great rates for world class services? We have variety of service offerings and options. At E & L Holdings we are totally committed to providing you low rates. With us you are guaranteed to get the best services and the best packages. A narrowing of the economy, locally led us to rethink our positioning strategy, that’s why we focus specifically on being a price sensitive company.

Mission

Bringing cost effective and measurable services to our clients by presenting only the highest level of professionalism and expertise, while ensuring that safety is prioritized throughout all facets of our business.

We demonstrate to our patrons that providing safety, dependable and efficient service systems is our main goal and by so doing we guarantee consistency every time.


Vision


- To develop an exceptional service delivery to enable our clients to rejoice

- To strive to continue being the best service provider in Gauteng province

- To provide excellent service to our clients, by giving them their principle position in the business and treating them with the integrity and honesty they     deserve

- To deliver quality service to our customers and value their money

- To train young professionals who have interest to work within the industry

Strategies

-No Surprises: Our price will be clearly communicated; there will be no surprises when you check the charges. We will always be there for you.     

-Complete business transparency: We do not offer “ad hoc” pricing or pricing du jour. We do not raise our prices to take advantage of seasonal demands. The price we quote over the phone or via e-mail will be the same price on your invoice.     

-Held to a high standard: We hold ourselves at a high standard, which shows in the service we provide hence we want to be held at a high standard. We would like to be known far and wide as the “gold standard” for our services in the industry.    

- A serious business that we take very seriously: This is a serious business and we try our best to understand our customers. We do a lot of the worrying so you don’t need to.
